About Us

StateQ Dynamics Pvt. Ltd. is a deep-tech startup incubated at the Technology Innovation Foundation of IIT Palakkad (TECHIN). We are committed to developing cutting-edge solutions in the domains of robotic systems, digital twins, and SDR-based transceiver systems.

With a focus on innovation and real-world impact, our technologies are engineered to bridge the gap between physical systems and their virtual counterparts, enabling smarter, more efficient, and adaptive automation solutions.

Our Vision

To pioneer intelligent, scalable systems that power the future of automation, communication, and simulation.

We believe in combining academic excellence with hands-on engineering to solve complex challenges and empower industries with next-generation technologies.
